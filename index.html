About Hack It Up

Hack It Up is a place where we learn to use latest technologies and tools.

We are passionate about latest tech and are inherently curious to try and learn anything that is new and trending. And what best way to learn something new, than hacking into it, getting our hands dirty, making quick workarounds and taking simpler route to get things working. Our approach is to learn and build projects with least effort and maximum exposure to best practices of the selected technologies and tools.

Here, we try to build things from scratch, taking readers along the exciting roller coaster ride of learning and great exposure, to end up with an “almost” real world prototype.

Have questions or suggestions? Feel free to mail us - info@thmedialabs.com.

Thanks for reading!
